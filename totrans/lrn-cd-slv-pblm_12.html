PROBLEM CREDITS

I’m grateful for the time and expertise offered by everyone who helps people learn through competitive programming. For each problem in this book, I have sought to identify its author and where the problem came from. If you have additional information or credits for any of the following problems, please let me know. Updates will be posted on the book’s website.
